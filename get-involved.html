Become an Ambassador

Ambassadors are community leaders who take our mission to their schools, workplaces, gyms, and neighborhoods. Whether you're a TBI survivor, family member, healthcare professional, or passionate advocate, as an Ambassador you become the face of TBI awareness in your community.

  • Lead or host awareness events in your community
  • Share educational resources with your network
  • Represent Hug Your Head at community gatherings
  • Receive official ambassador resources and recognition
  • Be part of a national network of TBI advocates
